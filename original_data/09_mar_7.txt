UNLAWFUL POSSESSION OF A WEAPON

3/6/2009 11:28:00 PM

Narrative:
RP, A PRINCETON UNIVERSITY STUDENT, OBSERVED A STUDENT AGED MALE CARRYING WHAT APPEARED TO
BE A RIFLE NEAR SPELMAN HALL WALKING TOWARDS PATTON HALL.  UNITS DISPATCHED.  PBPD DISPATCHED. 
OFFICERS SEARCHED THE AREA FOR THE SUSPECT.  A PTENS CAMPUS ALERT WAS SENT OUT TO THE
UNDERGRADUATE COMMUNITY.  AS A RESULT OF THE INVESTIGATION, A PRINCETON UNIVERSITY STUDENT
WAS LOCATED AND IDENTIFIED AS THE PERSON SEEN WITH THE WEAPON.  OFFICERS DETERMINED THAT THE
WEAPON WAS AN INOPERABLE FIREARM.  THE STUDENT WAS SUBSEQUENTLY ARRESTED BY THE PBPD FOR
UNLAWFUL POSSESSION OF AN IMITATION FIREARM.  REPORT FILED; CASE REFERRED.
